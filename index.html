About Us

Chennai Pharma Consultants (CPC) has been consistently serving the pharmaceutical industries since 2003. Over the years, CPC, the leading consulting firm offers turnkey solutions to the pharmaceutical industry globally.

CPC provides architectural and engineering services to the Pharmaceutical, Chemicals Industries. We are a turnkey project consultant offering various services from scratch up to start-up, erection & commissioning of new pharmaceutical plants. Our vision is to provide economic & state of art pharmaceutical unit with compliance of international standard to our clients. We experience in more than 50 pharmaceuticals units globally.

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

CPC offers services such as Project Management through Team Work systematic approach as listed - product forecast, estimation of production capacity, project mission assignment, design concept & conceptual drawings, development of process flow, development of room data sheet, develop project validation master plan, equipment listing, sourcing of equipment’s and documentation for URS, FDS, IQ, OQ, PQ, FAT, SAT etc.
